My Latest Work

This is the website I am currently working on. My cousin has started her very own thrifting business, and asked me if I could design a website for her. I have just started so we're still in the initiation phase.

I began creating a brand first. She shared the Pinterest board she had made with me, and the colors that stood out to me the most where purple/lilac, yellow/mustard and black/grey. From there on I made a palette and three different sets of logo's. She picked her favourite, and I made two alternatives based off of that one. Then I started looking for fonts, and I ran every single on of these fonts by her until we found two that she liked.
